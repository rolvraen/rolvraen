Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application Today: Sun, Weather & Facts (also known as Dagen i Dag), developed by Strong Resolve ("we", "us", "our").

1. Information We Collect

The app requests access to your device's location (GPS) for the sole purpose of calculating accurate sunrise, sunset, twilight times, and retrieving local weather data. Location data is processed entirely on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

If you deny location permission, the app will default to Oslo, Norway for all calculations.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — displays banner advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Norwegian Meteorological Institute (api.met.no, frost.met.no) — weather data is fetched using your approximate location. See MET Terms of Service.
  • Wikimedia API (api.wikimedia.org) — historical events are fetched from Wikipedia. No personal data is sent. See Wikimedia Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require user accounts, registration, or login. All astronomical calculations are performed locally on your device.

4. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Sources & Licensing

  • Weather data: Norwegian Meteorological Institute — NLOD / CC BY 4.0
  • Historical events: Wikipedia — CC BY-SA 4.0
  • Astronomy calculations: Based on NOAA solar equations, computed locally

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
